A design system focused on web interfaces. Built from scratch for the Ampire cyber range, later adopted by the AmTIP and IM products.

- Dark and light themes: every component is adapted for both
- Customizable color schemes built on tokens
- Flexible components tailored to the current product
- Desktop-first
- Based on Vuetify

Published in the Figma Community.
